About Slope Emoji

Slope Emoji is an arcade runner where classic slope gameplay meets a world of emojis, emotions, and endless speed! This isn’t just another rolling ball game — this is your chance to guide your favorite emoji through a chaotic neon slope packed with surprises.

What Is Slope Emoji?

Slope Emoji is a creative spin on the popular Slope series. Instead of a basic ball, you control a bouncing emoji head (smiley, cool face, heart-eyes, and more) as it races down a twisting, tilting 3D slope. 

Controls

Use the arrow keys to steer your emoji left and right.

Avoid falling off the edge of the slope or crashing into red blocks.

The longer you survive, the faster you go and the higher your score climbs.

Collect stars or power-ups along the way (in supported versions) to boost your score or unlock new emoji faces!
